About Me

Hello! I'm Vincent Kiplangat Sang Masway, a passionate tech enthusiast, educator, and author dedicated to making a difference in the world through innovation and technology. With a strong background in Artificial Intelligence, web development, and software engineering, I thrive on creating solutions that tackle real-world challenges.

I am the founder of KIITE Inspirations, an initiative that focuses on empowering individuals through knowledge, inspiration, innovation, transformation, and empowerment (KIITE). We aim to foster resilience and personal growth by providing resources, mentorship, and support for those seeking to achieve their dreams.

Additionally, I lead Kimaswai Global Destiny Network Africa, a platform designed to connect and uplift young people across Africa. Our mission is to create a network of support, encouragement, and collaboration among youth, helping them navigate challenges and discover their potential. We believe in the power of community and mentorship to inspire positive change and transformation.

My journey hasn't always been easy; I grew up in a humble family and faced numerous challenges along the way. However, these experiences shaped my character and fueled my passion for personal growth and community development. I firmly believe that with the right mindset and determination, we can overcome any obstacles that life presents.

In addition to my technical skills, I’m an avid motivational speaker and writer, constantly seeking opportunities to share my knowledge and inspire others. I have authored books that aim to change mindsets and promote personal development, and I actively engage in mentorship programs to support young talents in my community.

In my free time, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading inspiring books, or volunteering to help those in need. I’m excited to connect with like-minded individuals and collaborate on innovative projects that drive positive change.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope to connect with you soon!
